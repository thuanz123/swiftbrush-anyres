SwiftBrush-AnyRes: One-Step Text-to-Image Diffusion Models with Any-Resolution

Thuan Hoang Nguyen
VinAI Research
Paper arXiv Video Code Data

SwiftBrush-AnyRes can generate images at any resolution within one-step.

Abstract

Text-to-image synthesis has witnessed significant advancements in recent years, particularly with the advent of diffusion models. These models, notably one-step text-to-image diffusion models, offer a promising approach to generate high-fidelity images from textual descriptions in a single step. This paper provides a comprehensive overview of one-step text-to-image diffusion models, elucidating their underlying principles, architecture, and training methodologies. We delve into the unique characteristics and advantages of these models, such as their ability to synthesize diverse and realistic images conditioned on textual prompts efficiently. Specifically, we introduce SwiftBrush-AnyRes, a novel one-step text-to-image diffusion model that incorporates adaptive resolution control for enhanced image synthesis. Furthermore, we explore the challenges and limitations associated with one-step text-to-image diffusion models, including scalability and computational complexity. Through a critical analysis of existing research, we highlight key advancements, applications, and future directions in this rapidly evolving field. This paper aims to serve as a valuable resource for researchers and practitioners interested in text-to-image synthesis and diffusion models.
